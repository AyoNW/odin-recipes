Jollof Rice

Description

A spicy, smoky, sweet rice dish, flavoured with tomatoes, bell peppers and scotch bonnets, among various seasonings.

A favourite among various West African countries, this dish originated in Senegal.

Ingredients

  • Long grain rice
  • Red bell peppers
  • Red onions
  • Tinned plum tomatoes
  • Scotch bonnets
  • Curry powder
  • Bay leaves
  • Chicken stock
  • Maggi seasoning

Method

  1. Grill bell peppers, onions and scotch bonnets until charred.
  2. Blend bell peppers, onions and scotch bonnets with tinned tomatoes until smooth liquid is formed.
  3. Pour oil into pot and fry this liquid until the colour deepens and water begins to evaporate - this will splatter.
  4. Season generously.
  5. Pour in chicken stock.
  6. Pour washed rice into this mixture.
  7. Cover rice with foil and a lid.
  8. Allow to cook on low for as long as it takes.
