Cornflake Chicken

Description

A simple, comforting dish - juicy chicken tenders encrusted in seasoned cornflakes for extra crunch.

Can be air fried, deep fried or baked!

Ingredients

  • Chicken tenders/breasts
  • Cornflakes
  • Plain flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt/pepper/various seasonings

Method

  1. Slice chicken into preferred size - tenders, strips, nuggets, etc
  2. Prepare 3 bowls - one with flour, one with beaten eggs, one with cornflakes
  3. Generously season each bowl
  4. Dip chicken into flour, then eggs, then breadcrumbs. Ensure chicken pieces are fully coated.
  5. Air fry, fry or bake
